The Lighting Theif

Published: 2005
Genre: Fantasy,Middle grade
Rating: 5/5
Summary
The Lightning Thief is the first book in the series. Percy finds out hes a demigod. SO he goes to Camp Half-Blood. On the way, his mom also gets disintegrated. Anyway, we learn he's a son of Poseidon. which is a BIG deal because the Big Three arent supposed to have kids. His mom also gets disintegrated on the way to camp. Someone steals Zeus lightning bolt, and EVERYONE thinks Percy did it. He goes on a quest with Annabeth(my favorite) and Grover, and ends up facing off with Luke, who was actually the thief. Oh, and Kronos is kinda behind it all. And he gets his mom back
Review
Again, I am biased because this book got me back into reading, and for that reason, I love it. Also, this book was super fast-paced, and it held my attention span, which was great. I also loved how the characters are around my age, too.

    • The Sea of Monsters

    Published: 2006
    Genre: Fantasy, middle grade
    Rating: 5/5
    Summary
    The Sea of Monsters is the second book, and after Luke left camp, he poisoned Thalia's tree. And so as a result, the magical borders are dying. Percy finds out he has a cyclops half-brother, Tyson. They go to the Sea of Monsters to get the Golden Fleece. Luke is back, still evil, and Kronos is literally in a coffin. They get back to camp with the fleece in time at the end, and accidentally bring Thalia back from the dead
    Review
    I will say this book is relatively calm and the least angsty of the series. I LOVE the siren scene between Annabeth and Percy. ( Or should I say Percabeth). Other than that, I loved the blooming friendship between Clarise and Percy. Tyson was also an amazing character. I also adored the fatal flaw aspects because it was something we could all relate to.

    • The Titans Curse by Rick Riordan

    Published: 2007
    Genre: Fantasy, Middle grade
    Rating: 5/5
    Summary
    In The Titans Curse, we finally meet Thalia (now no longer a tree ), and Bianca and Nico di Angelo. Bianca becomes a Hunter of Artemis at age 12. And then Artemis and Annabeth get kidnapped. Percy sneaks onto a quest to help save his future girlfriend. Bianca dies, and we find Annabeth holding the sky instead of Atlas. Percy then hold the sky while people fight. Then a Big showdown on a mountain happens (RIP Zoey Nightshade). Luke jumps off a cliff. We save Artemis and Annabeth. Atlas is back to holding the sky. Nico finds out he's a son of Hades AND learns his sister died.
    Reiview
    This book was again super good. I love how Percy was slowly starting to get sick of this and just wanted Annabeth back. I also loved how Percy and Thalia's dynamic started to grow. My favorite scene was the Hoover Dam scene. I also loved Bessie, the sea cow.

      • The Last Olympain

      Published: 2009
      Genre: Fantasy, middle grade
      Rating: 5/5
      Summary
      The Last Olympian is the big finale. Kronos has full power and attacks Manhattan. Percy finally learns the big prophecy. They all fight, tons of deaths. Luke ends up being the hero in the end by sacrificing himself. Percy turns down immortality for Annabeth( aww, how sweet), and for once, Percy never looks back. And yes, Percy and Annabeth have the BEST underwater kiss ever!!
      Review
      OMG, this was such an incredible ending. The battle of Olympus was so sad that I definitely cried. It felt like we were losing character after character. It was wild, but either way, I have a love for this series in my heart. (Writing this review reminds me I need to read Heroes of Olympus) This series also gave me a love for Greek mythology and stories. This book definitely had tons of angst, but either way, it was incredible.
