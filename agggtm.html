This book follows 17-year-old Pippa Fitz-Amobi(Pip). Pip has a Capstone Project or EPQ ( depending on the US or UK version you read). Pip has decided that Sal Singh is innocent and didn't kill his girlfriend, Andie Bell ( By the way, Sal is also dead). ( Spoiler alert, she's right! *gasp*) During the process of trying to solve the case. She befriends Sals' brother Ravi. In the end, we find out Elliot Ward( who is Pip's friend's dad, and also teacher). Accidentally gave Andie a concussion. And then Andie's sister, Becca Bell, just watches her die. Becca then shoves her into a tank. Pip finds out this information and gets drugged by Becca Bell. She Survives, and Elliot goes to Jail
