Renegades by Marissa Myers

Published: 2017
Genre: Young Adult,Science Fiction,Dystopian
Rating: 5/5
Summary
Renegades by Marrisa Myers is about Nova in a city with superheroes called the Renegades who rule as both heroes and government. Nova Artino does not like the renegades because they did not save her parents and sister, who were killed. She ends up becoming a renegade, which is a part of her plan to overthrow the renegades. In the end, she ends up killing one of the villains, saving the day. While also falling in love on the way.
Review
This book was VERY slow in the beginning, so it definitely took a while for me to get into it. After I did, I could not put it down. I loved the blooming romance between Adrian and Nova. Speaking of Nova, I think she was a good character with motivation, which is important. The ending also had me shocked. I genuinely thought Ace Anarchy was dead. As someone on the second book, I can say that the pace picked up a little bit. Though it still follows the slow-paced to fast-paced ratio.
