Review
This book is now one of my favorites in the series. I really love Haymitches charecter, he had a lot of attitude and audacity. Which worked well not only for his "rascal" charecter for the capatol ,but also genuinley for making him a good charecter. This book along with almost every Hunger Games book was sad. EXTEREAMLY SAD I cried a good amount while reading, or wanted to cry. I will say I dont remember much of what I didnt like about this book which is crazy. I will add I do wish we got more Lenore Dove in someway,I didnt really cry at her death,because I wasnt super attached to her. In the end I still love the Hunger games so much and didn't mind this book.
